# Introducing Giftflow

**Giftflow** is a powerful WordPress plugin designed to streamline donation management for non-profit organizations, charities, and fundraising initiatives. Built with modern technology and user experience in mind, Giftflow provides everything you need to create and manage a successful donation website.

## The Complete Donation Solution

Managing donations shouldn't be complicated. Giftflow brings together all the essential tools in one comprehensive plugin, allowing you to focus on what matters most—your mission and your donors.

### Everything You Need in One Place

Giftflow provides a complete ecosystem for donation management, eliminating the need for multiple plugins or complicated integrations. From campaign creation to donor relationship management, payment processing to email communications—it's all built in and works seamlessly together.

## Key Features

### Campaign Management
Create and manage multiple fundraising campaigns with ease. Set goals, track progress in real-time, and showcase your impact with beautiful, customizable campaign pages.

### Donor Management
Build lasting relationships with your supporters. Track donor history, view contribution patterns, and maintain detailed records of every supporter who believes in your cause.

### Donation Processing
Accept donations effortlessly with our streamlined donation forms. Donors can contribute in minutes with a smooth, intuitive checkout experience that works perfectly on any device.

### Flexible Payment Methods
Accept payments your way with built-in support for:
- **Stripe** - Credit and debit card processing
- **PayPal** - Trusted worldwide payment solution
- **Local Bank Direct** - Direct bank transfers for your region

Setup is simple and straightforward, with no hidden fees or complicated configurations.

### Smart Email System
Stay connected with your donors through automated email notifications. Send instant donation receipts, campaign updates, and thank you messages—all customizable to match your organization's voice. 

### Security First
Your donors' trust is paramount. Giftflow is built with security as a top priority:
- Secure payment processing with industry-standard encryption
- PCI compliance through trusted payment gateways
- Regular security updates and best practices
- Data protection and privacy controls

## Technical Requirements

Giftflow is built on modern WordPress standards to ensure reliability and performance:

- **WordPress Version**: 6.0 or higher
- **PHP Version**: 8.2 or higher
- **MySQL**: 5.7 or higher (or MariaDB 10.3+)
- **HTTPS**: Recommended for payment processing
